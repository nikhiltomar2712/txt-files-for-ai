1) Concept
Neural networks learn by adjusting weights using gradients computed from a loss function.

2) Core flow (Python)
- Prepare data batches
- Forward pass: model(x)
- Compute loss: loss_fn(preds, targets)
- Backward pass: loss.backward()
- Optimizer step: optimizer.step()

3) Why it matters
This structure is the backbone of training any AI model in PyTorch/TensorFlow-like workflows.

4) Minimal training-loop skeleton
```python
for epoch in range(1):
    for xb, yb in loader:
        optimizer.zero_grad()
        preds = model(xb)
        loss = loss_fn(preds, yb)
        loss.backward()
        optimizer.step()
```
