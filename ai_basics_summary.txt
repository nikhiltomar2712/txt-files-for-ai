AI & LLM quick summary (text fallback).

1) AI: broad term for systems that perform tasks requiring intelligence.
2) ML: trains models from data.
3) DL: neural networks with multiple layers.
4) LLMs: large language models trained to predict the next token in text.
5) Transformer: architecture based on self-attention.
6) Training loop: forward pass -> compute loss -> backprop -> optimizer step.
7) Inference: generate next token repeatedly using a sampling strategy.
